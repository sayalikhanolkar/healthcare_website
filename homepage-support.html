Frequently Asked Questions

How often should I exercise?

It is recommended to exercise at least 3-5 times a week for optimal health. Incorporate a mix of cardio, strength training, and flexibility exercises.

Whatâ€™s the best time to meditate?

Meditation can be done at any time, but many find it beneficial in the morning to start the day with clarity or before bed to relax and unwind.

How much water should I drink daily?

It is generally recommended to drink 8 glasses (about 2 liters) of water a day. However, individual needs may vary based on activity level and climate.

What are the benefits of a balanced diet?

A balanced diet provides essential nutrients, improves energy levels, supports immune function, and reduces the risk of chronic diseases.

How can I improve my sleep quality?

To improve sleep quality, maintain a consistent sleep schedule, create a relaxing bedtime routine, and avoid screens before bed.

What are some tips for stress management?

Practice mindfulness, exercise regularly, maintain a healthy diet, and ensure you get enough sleep. Talking to a friend or professional can also help.
