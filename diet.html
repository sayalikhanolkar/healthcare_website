To maintain your weight, you need to eat the same number of calories as you burn in a day. If you want to lose weight, eat about 500 to 750 calories less than your daily need. This helps you lose around 0.5 to 1 kg per week. Eating healthy foods like proteins, fruits, and vegetables, along with exercise, makes it easier. To gain weight, eat 300 to 500 more calories than your daily need. Focus on foods like nuts, whole grains, and lean proteins, and do strength training to build muscle, not just gain fat.
